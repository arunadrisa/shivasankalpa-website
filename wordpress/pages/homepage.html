With the blessings of the Ubhaya Jagadgurus of Dakshinamnaya Sri Sharada Peetham, Sringeri

We don't run a Gurukulam. We ensure they thrive.

Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda is a trust dedicated to the support and upliftment of Vedic Gurukulas across India. We identify their needs, create awareness about their invaluable work, generate resources through community engagement, and help them grow sustainably.

We are the bridge between those who cherish Vedic Dharma and the Gurukulas that keep it alive.

How We Serve

🔍

Identify Needs

We work closely with Veda Gurukulas to understand their real challenges — from infrastructure gaps and faculty sustenance to student welfare.

🪔

Create Awareness

Through sacred events, community outreach, and storytelling, we bring the world of Gurukulas — their dedication and significance — to a wider audience.

🙏

Generate Support

We mobilize resources — financial, material, and human — so that Gurukulas can focus on what they do best: preserving and transmitting the Vedas.

🌱

Foster Growth

From supporting Vedic research and building libraries to aiding Gośālā development, we nurture the entire ecosystem around Vedic learning.

"The particles of earth may be counted, the drops of rain may be counted. But even Brahmā, the Creator, cannot measure the fruits of Vedādhyayana."

For millennia, the Guru–Shishya Paramparā has preserved the Vedas through an unbroken chain of oral transmission — a living heritage unlike anything else in the world. Today, Gurukulas across India carry this sacred responsibility. Young students dedicate years of their lives to mastering the Vedas, guided by Adhyapakas who have themselves walked this path.

Yet many of these Gurukulas operate quietly, with limited resources and little public recognition. They need our collective support — not just to survive, but to flourish.
