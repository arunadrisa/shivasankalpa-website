Your Offering Sustains a Timeless Tradition

Every contribution — large or small — helps a Vedic Gurukula continue its sacred mission of preserving the Vedas through the Guru–Shishya Paramparā.

Why Your Support Matters

Vedic Gurukulas are among the last living institutions where the Vedas are transmitted exactly as they have been for thousands of years — through dedicated oral learning between Guru and Shishya. Students spend years mastering the sacred texts, guided by Adhyapakas who have themselves walked this path.

Yet many Gurukulas face real, everyday challenges: maintaining infrastructure, supporting teachers' livelihoods, providing for students' basic needs, and sustaining operations with little external help.

Your donation directly supports:

Vedic Education

Learning materials, infrastructure, and student welfare in Gurukulas

Adhyapaka Welfare

Recognition and sustenance for teachers who dedicate their lives to this tradition

Sacred Events

Gatherings that bring communities together and generate awareness

Gurukula Growth

Vedic libraries, research, and Gośālā support for long-term development

Seva Opportunities

Mahā Rudra Puraścaraṇa — May 15–17, 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-exempt under Section 80G?

Our 80G registration is currently being processed. Once approved, all eligible donations will qualify for tax exemption, and we will issue certificates accordingly.

How will my donation be used?

Donations support Vedic Gurukula education, Adhyapaka welfare, sacred events like the Mahā Rudra Puraścaraṇa, and trust operations. We are committed to publishing utilization reports.

Yes. Choose from the seva options listed above, or mention your preferred seva when sharing your transaction details at info@shivasankalpa.org.

International donation options are being set up. In the meantime, please write to us at info@shivasankalpa.org and we will assist you personally.

Yes. Please email your transaction details to info@shivasankalpa.org, and we will send you a confirmation receipt.
