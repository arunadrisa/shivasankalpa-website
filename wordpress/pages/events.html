Events & Initiatives

Sacred gatherings that bring together devotees, scholars, and supporters in service of Vedic Dharma.

Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda organizes and supports events that serve two purposes: to perform sacred Vedic rituals for the welfare of all, and to bring visibility and resources to the Gurukulas that preserve our ancient traditions.
