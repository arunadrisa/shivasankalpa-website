About Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda

A trust born from the conviction that Vedic Gurukulas deserve our unwavering support.

Why We Exist

Across India, Vedic Gurukulas quietly sustain one of humanity's greatest intellectual and spiritual traditions. Within their modest walls, young students rise before dawn to learn the Vedas through the same oral tradition that has been practiced for thousands of years. Adhyapakas — teachers who have dedicated their own lives to mastering the Vedas — guide each student with patience and devotion.

Yet these Gurukulas rarely make headlines. Many operate with limited funding, aging infrastructure, and almost no public visibility. The teachers who give their lives to this tradition are often the least recognized.

Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda was founded to change this.

We are not a Gurukulam. We are a trust that exists to ensure Gurukulas thrive. We identify what they need, bring their story to a wider community, mobilize resources, and work alongside them to build a sustainable future for Vedic education.

Our name comes from the Shukla Yajurveda — "तन्मे मनः शिवसंकल्पमस्तु" — "May our minds be filled with auspicious resolve." This resolve is our foundation: to serve the Gurukulas that preserve the Vedas, and to invite others to join us in this sacred responsibility.

Our Vision

A world where every Vedic Gurukula has the resources, recognition, and community it needs to thrive — and where the Guru–Shishya Paramparā continues to illuminate generations.

Our Mission

To identify the needs of Veda Gurukulas, create public awareness about their vital role in preserving Vedic Dharma, generate financial and community support, and foster their long-term growth and sustainability.

What We Work Towards

Vedic Education & Saṁskāra

Support Vedic education in Gurukulas, fostering cultural values and the cultivation of traditional arts and skills.

Community Awareness

Foster community engagement through awareness programs, workshops, and outreach initiatives.

Vedic Research & Libraries

Support Vedic research initiatives and help build Vedic libraries that serve scholars and students alike.

Financial Sustenance

Provide direct financial assistance to Veda Gurukulas for their operational and developmental needs.

Honoring Vedic Scholars

Recognize the quiet sacrifice of Vedic scholars and Adhyapakas who have dedicated their lives to preserving the Gurukula tradition.

Gośālā & Go-samrakṣaṇam

Support the development of Gośālās and the noble cause of indigenous cow protection, integral to Vedic life.

With the Blessings of the Ubhaya Jagadgurus

Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda carries out its mission with the boundless grace and divine blessings of His Holiness Jagadguru Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahasannidhanam and His Holiness Jagadguru Sri Sri Vidhushekhara Bharati Sannidhanam of Dakshinamnaya Sri Sharada Peetham, Sringeri.

This sacred guidance inspires every initiative we undertake and reminds us that our work is in service of a tradition far greater than ourselves.
