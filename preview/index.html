About Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda

A trust born from the conviction that Vedic Gurukulas deserve our unwavering support.

Why We Exist

Across India, Vedic Gurukulas quietly sustain one of humanity's greatest intellectual and spiritual traditions. Within their modest walls, young students rise before dawn to learn the Vedas through the same oral tradition that has been practiced for thousands of years.

Yet these Gurukulas rarely make headlines. Many operate with limited funding, aging infrastructure, and almost no public visibility.

Sri Shivasankalpa Vṛnda was founded to change this.

We are not a Gurukulam. We are a trust that exists to ensure Gurukulas thrive. Our name comes from the Shukla Yajurveda — "तन्मे मनः शिवसंकल्पमस्तु" — "May our minds be filled with auspicious resolve."

Our Vision

A world where every Vedic Gurukula has the resources, recognition, and community it needs to thrive — and where the Guru–Shishya Paramparā continues to illuminate generations.

Our Mission

To identify the needs of Veda Gurukulas, create public awareness about their vital role, generate financial and community support, and foster their long-term growth.
